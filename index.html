About Calculator

SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average): SGPA is a measure of a student's academic performance in a given semester. It is calculated by taking the weighted average of grade points obtained in all the courses registered by the student during the semester.

Formula for SGPA:

\( \text{SGPA} = \frac{{\sum (C_i \times G_i)}}{{\sum C_i}} \)
where \( C_i \) is the number of credits for the \( i \)-th course and \( G_i \) is the grade point obtained in the \( i \)-th course.

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average): CGPA is a measure of a student's academic performance across all semesters completed so far. It is calculated by taking the weighted average of the SGPA of all the semesters.

Formula for CGPA:

\( \text{CGPA} = \frac{{\sum (S_j \times C_j)}}{{\sum C_j}} \)
where \( S_j \) is the SGPA of the \( j \)-th semester and \( C_j \) is the total number of credits in that semester.
